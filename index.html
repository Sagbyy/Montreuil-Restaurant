For the design of the website, we had divided the tasks. Each member of the group has a page to develop. Andreas is in charge of the Journey, Ayoub of the data protection, Youssef to list the restaurants that we had listed with Trello and me Salah I took care to develop the homepage, the navbar, anchor buttons and the footer. We helped each other to move forward more quickly, unfortunately we did not have time to complete all the objectives of the project but we are very happy to have finished the main objectives !
